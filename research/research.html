Swasner - The Next Gen PPE

A smart personal Protective Equipment , capable of providing you a Personal space of purified air, making your life hasslefree away from those tight masks around your face.

We have redesigned how conventional PPE should look, aiming to mark this as a "mainstream Protective Gear". While renovating the PPE, we emphasised on

  1. 1. Design Aesthetics
  2. 2. Ventilation and Perspiration problem inside the PPE
  3. 3. Improving the longevity of product
  4. 4. Ease of wearing

Quaint but Enticing Design

Working in preProduction for more than 5 months, our design team was able to foster the quirkiest PPE you could have ever seen. Packed with features which users could only imagine of to be in a Protective Gear.

From wearing an entire suit of plastic, we knew which part of the body needed to be protected. Cutting off the avoidable clothing we made Swasner not only economical but also fashionable. We didn't want to bring something obscure in front of people thus tried to make the design akin to just another hoodie.

We kept the shoulder short for restriction free movement. To avoid any leaks our product comes with stretchable elastics on shoulder and waist cuts. In front we kept opening with gloves attached inside, to access the face without breaking the bio-bubble.

End of Sweating inside PPE

We knew how it felt to wear a complete airlocked suit the entire day, no alternative for the sweat to dry up. Thus getting inspired from our previous celebrated product "bubble Helmet : for non Invasive ventilation" to create a positive air pressure of purified air around the person for ease in respiration and reducing perspiration to rock bottom.

We have procured the best in class fan in this price segment, with attractive specs on the datasheet :

Features Details
RPM (Rotation Per Minute) 1200
Bearing ( refers to the ball bearing inside the armature) SSO (Self Stabilising Oil Pressure Bearing) having a lifespan of 150,000 hours of running
Acoustic Noise 18.1 dB(A) ( Lower the decibel, lower is the noise)
Max Static Pressure 1.31 mmH2O

We performed several simulations to find the optimum position for the placement of the fan. We took into consideration the heat flow diagram and aerodynamics of our product.

Immuned to wear and tear

Swasner was meant to last, and it was always our priority. We made prototypes wondering upon varied cloth materials .Factors we had to consider in this selection procedure were :

1. Imperviable : First target was to make swasner an all weather product, and for this we had to make it with a cloth material being hydrophobic in nature.

2. Breathable : This was our first barrier as both these features were contradictory to each other, so we tried to minimize the thickness of the fabric to the least. As our target audience were frontline workers, we needed to keep in mind about the heating issue inside Swasner.

3. Comfortable : As there were regions where the fabric came in direct contact with the skin, we improvised to make it comfortable by adding another layer of fabric inside. We tested different options like : mesh cloth, Astar etc.

4. Fashionable : And our ultimate aim was to break the stereotype of PPE. This could have only been done by making Swasner a fashion statement. Our team after several iterations accepted the fabric which survived in all the requirements and still had an aesthetic feature to it.

Easy in ,Easy Out

Those who have tried wearing a PPE suit, know that wearing it is itself another big Task. Thus we made this intelligent design in which you could wear it under 14 seconds.
